Black Rot Solution

Cause: A fungal disease caused by *Botryosphaeria obtusa*.

Solution:

  • Prune infected branches.Fungicides with the active ingredient Myclobutanil are most effective in preventing rust.
  • Apply copper-based fungicides.Copper and sulfur products can be used as well. Spray trees and shrubs when flower buds first emerge until spring weather becomes consistently warm and dry.
  • Remove and destroy infected fruits.This disease can cause damage to leaves and fruit of very susceptible apple varieties but is only a minor problem on resistant or partially resistant trees. Do not plant eastern red cedar and juniper within a few hundred yards of apples, hawthorns, and other plants from the Rosaceae family. Disease-resistant varieties are available for some plants.
